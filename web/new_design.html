Frequently Asked Questions

Is my data secure?

Absolutely. We use Google Firebase Authentication and secure cloud storage to ensure your data is encrypted and safe.

Can I use Split Trip offline?

Yes! You can add expenses and view trips offline. The app will automatically sync everything once you reconnect to the internet.

Do all members need the app?

For the best experience, yes. However, you can add "offline members" to a trip manually if they don't have the app installed.

Which platforms are supported?

Currently, we are available on Android. An iOS version is in development and coming soon!
